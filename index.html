About Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is located on the west coast of India. It's a city of contrasts: from gleaming skyscrapers to traditional bazaars, from Bollywood glitz to slum resilience. Known as the City of Dreams, Mumbai is home to over 20 million people and an endless energy that drives the nation.
